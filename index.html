Research
The Latent Truth: Knowledge-Behavior Gap in Logical Deduction via Activation Steering
Harika Mahapatra
ICLR Workshop (under review)

Large language models often produce incorrect outputs despite internally encoding the correct answer. This work formalizes this as a knowledge-behavior gap and shows that latent representations encode truth early, but fail at decoding. I introduce a directional activation steering method that recovers a significant portion of logical failures without degrading coherence.

HRLS: Interpretable Temporal Prediction for Clinical Decision Support
Harika Mahapatra
NeurIPS WiML Workshop · IEEE Global AI Summit

Designed hybrid temporal architectures for clinical prediction across multi-disease datasets. Introduced Temporal Explanation Robustness (TER), a metric to evaluate stability of explanations over time for reliable deployment in healthcare settings.
